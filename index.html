Kiss Heart Cupid - Rock Paper Scissors With A Romantic Twist

Anniversary Style

Instructions:

  1. You will be playing against an evil love villian who is out to destroy romance.
  2. In order to defeat the villian you must win the most times out of three games. Should you fail, the villian will eliminate romance from the world forever.
  3. You may enter "Kiss," "Heart," or "Cupid."
  4. Kiss beats cupid, heart beats kiss, and cupid beats paper.
  5. Good luck! And remember, whoever wins the most out of three games is the official winner!
